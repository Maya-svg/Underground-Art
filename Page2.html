The Founder

The Underground Art Project started as a few local artists helping each other sell their pieces through through family and friends. "From three artists in 2016 to over fifty artists succesffully creating, selling, and mentoring through the Underground Art Project, we have expanded beyond my wildest dreams, states founder Harnaljia. This is not like any other online galleries. Underground Art Project is a community base program that helps rising artists expand their skill set, gain valuable exposure, and make a change in their communities. Thank you for visiting our online gallery and we would love to see you in person at our pop up gallery events through out the country.
